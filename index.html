Who are we?

We are one community of like-minded individuals who follow the teachings and beliefs brought to us by Santa Claus. If you let Him, He will fill your soul up with joy and comfort all year round.

If you're looking for or curious about alternative life guidance, as S.C. teaches, all you have to do is ask for it.
